background information regarding this song:

  • I had been going through a small gothic country phase whenever working on this song. I don't think this song is particularly gothic (the lyrics are in gibberish anyway), but I still worked to incorporate that sort of country sound to it at all.
  • I named this song what I did because I was going through ear fatigue after mixing it. I've had unilateral hearing loss since my childhood, but sometimes the hearing imbalance is more clear to me if I've been listening to too many loud sounds for an unreasonable amount of time.
