background information regarding this song:

  • This is not the first marylouing song ever, but it was the oldest one that I was willing to keep public for a majority of the time.
  • Looking back, this song existing is a miracle to me because of the fact that I managed to make it after only having used FL Studio continuously for around 8 months. Admittedly, I did use FL Studio for a month back in April 2018 or so, but I did not have a strong dedication to the program at the time. I had also used GarageBand consistently from mid-2021 to mid-2023, which I suppose could make this song's existence a little less impressive for some. Most of the music I made during these two intervals were very low quality, however. At the end of it all though, I think this song was only ever possible because of the fact that I sped through learning how to do sound design during my first few months on FL Studio.
