background information regarding this song:

  • When I first went into making this song, my intention was not to make something this mellow. I wanted to perform a beat exercise that was adjacent to Hard Dance and Dariacore (this explains the leroy vocal sample), but I couldn't exactly get the sound design down. After much trial and error, I eventually opted to slowing down the tempo, adding scratchy textures on the master through Infiltrator 2, and then messing around with synths that I had lying around in my plugin library that I never really bothered to use in advance.
  • This song made me feel nervous for a little while, as it had ended up becoming my most popular track for... a big portion of the time that marylouing was a thing. It ended up getting over 3,000 listens, with new listeners constantly coming in even at the twilight of this alias's existence. This was unbelievable to me considering the song had already been around for about a year and a half, and it got basically no attention for the first 6-8 months of being available, so the growth was more exponential than anything.
  • While I still appreciate this song of mine, I felt like it was constantly overshadowing everything else that I made after a certain point. This is especially considering that none of my other songs really sounded like how this one sounds like (at least to me).
